Pakistan's Leading Breakfast Cereal & FMCG Manufacturer

Bereket Foods is one of Pakistan's fastest-growing FMCG food manufacturers, headquartered in Islamabad with a state-of-the-art extrusion plant in Multan. Since our founding in 2022, we have grown from a vision of natural goodness into a multi-brand portfolio trusted by families across Pakistan and now positioning for international food export markets.

Our product range spans fortified breakfast cereals, instant porridges, premium jams and marmalades, digestive health solutions, and Basmati rice — all manufactured to international quality standards. As a Halal-certified food manufacturer in Pakistan, we meet the sourcing requirements of buyers across the Middle East, the United Kingdom, Canada, and beyond.
