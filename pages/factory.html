Halal-Certified Food Manufacturing Facility in Pakistan

Our breakfast cereal and FMCG manufacturing plant in Multan operates under strict food safety and hygiene standards, ensuring every product leaving our facility is safe, nutritious, and compliant with international regulations. As a Halal-certified food manufacturer in Pakistan, we supply to both domestic retail and international export markets, including buyers across the Middle East, Canada, and the UK.

Our extrusion technology produces consistent, high-quality breakfast cereals — from cornflakes and puffed wheat to chocolate-coated and fortified porridges — with batch traceability and quality checkpoints at every stage of production. We work with local grain farmers to source premium raw materials, supporting Pakistan's agricultural ecosystem while ensuring freshness and nutritional integrity.

Export-Ready Production & Private Label Capability

Bereket Foods' Multan plant is fully equipped for private label cereal production and food export from Pakistan. Our scalable production lines can accommodate both small and large-volume orders, with flexible packaging options to meet supermarket and importer specifications worldwide.

We maintain rigorous quality assurance protocols throughout the manufacturing process — from raw ingredient intake to finished-goods dispatch. Our facility is designed to meet the requirements of global retail buyers and regulators, making Bereket Foods a reliable contract manufacturing partner for food brands seeking a trusted, export-grade production base in South Asia.
