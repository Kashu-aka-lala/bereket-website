Why Choose Bereket Foods for Private Label Manufacturing?

As a specialized private label breakfast cereal and FMCG manufacturer in Pakistan, Bereket Foods brings together modern extrusion technology, in-house quality control, and flexible custom formulation capabilities. Our Multan-based manufacturing plant is export-ready, Halal-certified, and proven by successful partnerships with leading retail chains.

Modern Extrusion Plant

State-of-the-art cereal extrusion and coating lines in Multan capable of producing cornflakes, puffed grains, coated cereals, instant porridges, and more.

Halal Certified

All products are manufactured under Halal-compliant processes, making our private label solutions suitable for Middle Eastern, European, and North American market requirements.

Custom Formulation & Packaging

From recipe development and nutritional profiling to custom packaging design and label compliance — we manage the complete product development cycle for your brand.

Export Ready

With a presence in Pakistan and Canada, we understand international trade requirements and can facilitate food export from Pakistan to global markets efficiently.

Quality Assurance

Rigorous in-process and finished-product testing ensures consistency. Our quality standards satisfy leading regional supermarket supplier requirements including Carrefour.

Proven Track Record

Already manufacturing private label cereals for Carrefour and ChaseUp — two of Pakistan's largest retail networks — demonstrates our proven capacity and reliability for brand partners.

How Our Private Label Process Works

1
Inquiry & Brief
Share your product category, target market, nutritional requirements, and packaging preferences with our team at md@bereketfoods.com.
2
Custom Formulation
Our food technology team develops a product formula tailored to your taste, nutrition, and regulatory specs — cornflakes, porridge, coated cereals, granola, jams, and more.
3
Sampling & Approval
Pre-production samples are submitted for your review and approval before full-scale manufacturing begins.
4
Production & Quality Control
We manufacture to agreed specifications with full batch traceability and quality assurance at every stage of our Multan production facility.
5
Delivery & Export
Finished goods are delivered to your warehouse or handled for food export from Pakistan, with full documentation for Halal certification and customs compliance.
