Ethics & Compliance

Last Updated: February 2026

Bereket Foods is committed to conducting its business with the highest standards of integrity, ethics, and transparency. Our Ethics & Compliance program is designed to ensure that every employee and partner understands and adheres to our core values.

1. Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We do the right thing, even when no one is watching.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in everything we do.
  • Sustainability: We care for the environment and the communities we serve.
  • Respect: We value diversity and treat everyone with dignity.

2. Code of Conduct

Our Code of Conduct provides a framework for ethical decision-making. It applies to all employees, officers, and directors of Bereket Foods, as well as our subsidiaries and affiliates worldwide.

Key areas covered include:

  • Anti-corruption and anti-bribery
  • Fair competition and antitrust compliance
  • Conflicts of interest
  • Data privacy and protection
  • Workplace health and safety
  • Environmental stewardship

3. Supplier Responsibility

We expect our suppliers to share our commitment to ethical business practices. Our Supplier Code of Conduct establishes minimum standards for labor rights, environmental impact, and business integrity that all partners must meet.

4. Reporting Concerns

We encourage an open-door policy where employees and partners feel comfortable reporting any suspected violations of our Code of Conduct or applicable laws. Reports can be made through:

  • Direct management
  • Human Resources department
  • Legal and Compliance office
  • Our anonymous whistleblowing channel (compliance@bereketfoods.com)

Bereket Foods strictly prohibits retaliation against any individual who reports a concern in good faith.

5. Monitoring and Auditing

We regularly monitor and audit our operations to ensure compliance with our internal policies and external regulations. This includes periodic risk assessments and training programs for our employees.

6. Governance

The Ethics & Compliance program is overseen by our Board of Directors, which ensures that the company remains accountable to its stakeholders and the public.
